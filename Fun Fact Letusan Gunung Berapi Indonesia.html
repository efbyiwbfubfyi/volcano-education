VOLCANO




There are a several fun fact about volcano that you should know, is:

  • There are over 1,500 active volcanoes around the world today.
  • Most volcanoes are underwater, hidden beneath the ocean’s surface.
  • Volcanoes can create new islands, like those formed in the Pacific Ocean.
  • Lava can reach temperatures above 1,000°C, hot enough to melt rock.
  • Volcanic ash is not soft like fireplace ash it’s made of tiny, sharp rock particles.
  • Volcanic soil is very fertile, making areas around volcanoes great for farming.
  • The largest volcano in the solar system is on Mars, called Olympus Mons.
  • Lightning can occur during eruptions due to charged ash particles in the air.
